helpful note

Quick info before we get started — just a few things you might want to know.

What kind of website do you make?

I can develop corporate sites, landing pages, personal sites, and portfolio sites.

Anythings to prepare?

If you’ve got any text or images you’d like to include, sending them early makes things smoother. Especially images — they really shape the look and feel of the site. If possible, original photos are always better than stock ones!

How much will it cost?

It really depends on your project and what you’re looking for. But if you’ve got an idea, I’d love to hear it and let you know what it might cost!

How long does it take?

Designing a single/top page usually takes 2 to 4 weeks. Coding the design typically takes about 1 week per page.

Got any awards?

Not just yet — but I’m aiming for it someday. Right now, I’m focused on enjoying the process of making designs that align with my style, while also expanding what I can do along the way.
